Based in Vitoria ES and Cachoeiro de Itapemirim Brazil, we have over 20+ years combined experience in the Ornamental Stone Market.

We here at the Cornerstone Collection esteem luxury and quality above everything else. We strive for excellence and customer service and always support our clients supply needs.

The Cornerstone Collection is different from any other supplier in the market today. We have our own quarries and blocks. Which we process ourselves. Our regular line has the most popular colors in the market, but we are able to provide all the colors currently quarried in Brazil depending on our customers needs.
Whatever our clients need, we can provide it.

We specialize in the following services:
  • Large project supplier
  • Own Quarries
  • Block Sales
  • 2cm and 3cm
  • High end super exotics and rare materials.
  • Most popular colors in the market
  • Classic materials
  • High end marbles
  • Tiles and Pre-Fab
  • Landscape stones
  • Reoccuring Programs - reliabe and consistant

Our material line is a mixture of high-end showroom ready materials, with some of the most sought after colors in the market today.

We believe that every project needs its ideal foundation.
Let us be your foundation.
